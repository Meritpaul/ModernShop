Your satisfaction matters to us. If something isn't right with your order, here's how our return and refund process works.

Return Window

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of receiving your order, as long as the product is unused, in its original packaging, and with tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Innerwear, cosmetics, and personal care items (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page
  • Products that have been used, washed, or damaged by the customer

How to Request a Return

Contact us at {{ contact_email }} or call +880 1800-000000 with your order number and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the pickup or drop-off process.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval status. Approved refunds for bKash/Nagad payments are sent back to the same account within 3–5 working days. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are made via bKash/Nagad or bank transfer.

Damaged or Wrong Item Received

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.

Contact Us

For any return or refund questions, please contact us.
